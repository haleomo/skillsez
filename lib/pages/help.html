Help & FAQ

Find answers to common questions and get assistance with using Skills Ez.

  • How do I create a learning plan? Simply fill out the form on the home page with your learning profile and the skill you want to acquire, then submit it to generate your personalized plan.
  • Can I customize my learning plan? Yes, once you receive your plan, you can adjust the milestones and resources to better fit your schedule and preferences.
  • What if I need more help? If you have further questions or need assistance, feel free to reach out to our support team via the contact form on the About page.
